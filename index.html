Academic Programs

OBMC (O.B. Montessori Center) offers comprehensive Montessori education from preschool (Casa) through Senior High School, focusing on holistic development with strong academics, arts (music, theater, dance, visual arts), sports, and outdoor programs like scouting, all within nurturing, purpose-built environments to foster independence, creativity, and leadership. Their programs emphasize practical life skills, critical thinking, and community, with both traditional and flexible online/hybrid learning options available across various campuses in Metro Manila

Faculty and Staff

OBMC (Operation Brotherhood Montessori Center) staff includes leadership like President Sara Soliven-De Guzman, faculty (teachers), and service personnel (electricians, plumbers, cooks, housekeeping), all dedicated to the Montessori method, though employee reviews mention traditional management aspects and workload. They focus on creating a prepared environment for holistic student development, with ongoing training for all staff, from admin to maintenance.

Campus Facilities

O.B. Montessori Center (OBMC) has multiple campuses, each designed to offer a nurturing environment for learning. While specific details vary by location, the general facilities across the campuses include air-conditioned classrooms, libraries, art project areas, music theory instruction spaces, and facilities for public speaking and theater productions

Community and Values

We believe education extends beyond academics. Our school community emphasizes peace education, conflict resolution, and social responsibility. Students participate in community service projects, environmental initiatives, and cultural celebrations that foster global citizenship. Parent involvement is encouraged through regular conferences, classroom observations, volunteer opportunities, and educational workshops.

Admissions Information

We welcome applications throughout the year, with priority given to siblings of current students and returning families. The admissions process includes a school tour, classroom observation, and family interview to ensure alignment with Montessori principles. Financial assistance and flexible payment plans are available for qualified families. Schedule your visit today to experience our learning environment firsthand.
